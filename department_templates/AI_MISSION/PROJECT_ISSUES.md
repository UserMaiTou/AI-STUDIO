# PROJECT_ISSUES

## Purpose

AI maintains this file during project execution.

Use it to track blockers, risks, Human-waiting items, and unresolved follow-up work.

## What AI Must Record

- blockers
- risks
- items waiting for Human
- follow-up issues to resolve later

## Issue Entries

```text
Date:
Issue:
Type: blocker | risk | waiting-human | follow-up
Impact:
Current status:
Owner:
Needed decision or action:
Next check:
```
