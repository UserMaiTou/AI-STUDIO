# PROJECT_AI_FEEDBACK

## Purpose

AI maintains this file during and after project execution.

Use it for AI self-evaluation so Mission Pack upgrades are based on observed execution, not memory.

## What AI Must Evaluate

- which Mission Pack parts were effective
- which Mission Pack parts were ineffective
- what was missing
- what was excessive
- what AI would adjust if doing the project again
